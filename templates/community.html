Instavibe Community Guidelines
  1. Appropriate Imagery - We don’t allow nudity on Instavibe.
  2. Spam - This is not allowed on Instavibe (creating or submitting unwanted email, comments, likes or other forms of commercial or harassing communications).
  3. Illegal Content - We don’t allow support or praise of terrorism, organized crime or hate groups on Instavibe.
  4. Hate Speech, Bullying and Abuse - We remove credible threats of violence, hate speech and the targeting of private individuals.
  5. Graphic Violence - Graphic violence is not allowed and we may remove videos or images of intense, graphic violence to make sure that Instavibe stays appropriate for everyone.
  6. Any user found in violations of these guidelines, will have his/her account disabled indefinitely.
